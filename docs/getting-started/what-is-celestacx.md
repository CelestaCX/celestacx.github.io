### What is CelestaCX?
 
CelestaCX is an AI-powered omnichannel contact center platform that brings all your customer communication channels — voice calls, live chat, WhatsApp, email, social media, and video — into a single unified workspace for your agents and supervisors.
 
Rather than managing separate tools for each channel, CelestaCX gives your team one place to receive, handle, and track every customer interaction, regardless of how the customer reached out.
 
---
 
### Who is CelestaCX for?
 
CelestaCX is designed for organizations that run a customer-facing contact center — whether that's a small support team or a large enterprise operation. Within those organizations, different people use CelestaCX in different ways:
 | Role | What  they  do  in  CelestaCX |
| --- | --- |
| Agent | Handles  inbound  and  outbound  customer  conversations  across  all  channels  from  the  Agent  Desk |
| Supervisor | Monitors  live  agent  activity,  manages  teams,  reviews  dashboards,  and  assists  agents  in  real  time |
| Quality  Manager | Reviews  conversation  recordings,  scores  agent  performance,  and  runs  quality  evaluations |
| Admin | Configures  channels,  routing  rules,  user  accounts,  and  system  settings  via  the  Unified  Admin  panel |
| IT  /  DevOps | Deploys  and  maintains  the  CelestaCX  infrastructure  on  Kubernetes |
| Developer | Builds  custom  integrations,  Agent  Desk  implementations,  and  channel  connectors  using  CelestaCX  APIs |
| Partner | Resells  and  manages  CelestaCX  deployments  for  multiple  customer  organizations |

### What Can CelestaCX Do?
 
#### 🌐 Omnichannel Customer Engagement
 
Handle customer conversations across every major channel from one unified interface — including WhatsApp, Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Telegram, Viber, SMS, Email, Voice, and Web Chat. Agents see the full conversation history regardless of which channel the customer used.
 
#### 🤖 AI & Self-Service
 
Integrate NLU bots and conversational IVR flows to handle routine customer queries automatically, escalating to a live agent only when needed. CelestaCX supports popular bot frameworks including Rasa and Teneo.
 
#### 📞 Voice & Video
 
Make and receive voice calls natively through CelestaCX — including inbound routing, outbound dialing, call recording, and browser-based WebRTC calling. Video customer support is also available.
 
#### 🧭 Intelligent Routing
 
Route incoming conversations to the right agent or team based on skills, availability, priority, channel type, and business hours. Supports push-mode and pull-mode routing, queue prioritization, and precision routing rules.
 
#### 📊 Real-Time & Historical Reporting
 
Monitor contact center performance live through real-time dashboards, and analyse trends over time with historical reports covering agent productivity, queue performance, conversation volumes, and more.
 
#### 🔍 Quality Management
 
Record voice and screen activity, run structured evaluations using customizable scoring forms, and track quality trends across agents and teams with the built-in Quality Management module.
 
#### 🔒 Security & Compliance
 
CelestaCX is built with enterprise-grade security — including role-based access control, API gateway authentication, PII data masking, two-factor authentication, and compliance support for GDPR, HIPAA, and PCI DSS.
 
#### 🧩 Integrations & Extensibility
 
Connect CelestaCX to your existing CRM, workforce management system, or third-party tools. Developers can build custom Agent Desk implementations and channel connectors using documented APIs and SDK libraries.
 
---
 
### How CelestaCX is Deployed
 
CelestaCX runs on **Kubernetes** — an industry-standard container orchestration platform — which means it can be deployed on-premises, in your private cloud, or on a public cloud provider. This gives your organization full control over your data and infrastructure.
 
For partners managing multiple customers, CelestaCX 5.0 supports **multi-tenancy** — meaning a single CelestaCX deployment can serve multiple isolated customer organizations from the same underlying infrastructure.
