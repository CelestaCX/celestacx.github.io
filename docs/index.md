## About CelestaCX

CelestaCX is [Octavebytes'](https://octavebytes.com) AI-powered omnichannel contact center platform. It unifies every customer communication channel — voice, chat, WhatsApp, email, social media, and video — into a single workspace for agents and supervisors.

Built on Kubernetes, CelestaCX is designed for enterprise-scale deployments, supporting multi-tenancy, high availability, and integration with leading CRM and workforce management tools.
